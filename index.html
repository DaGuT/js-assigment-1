You can build parallelogram and circle by only placing 3 points on the left part of the screen! Center of the circle is based on center of mass of parallelogram

  1. Click at any place of the left part
  2. Place two more points
  3. Check information on the right side

You can also move points around by drag-n-drop

Press "Reset" to remove all points and figures

Even though we can build plenty of parallelograms based on 3 points, We only stick with one

Square of parallelogram is calculated based on Geron's formula for trinagles using three known points (as double of that square)
